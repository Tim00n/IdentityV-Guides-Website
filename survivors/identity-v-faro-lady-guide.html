IdentityV Faro Lady Guide

Classification and Type

Faro Lady is a Decoder with above average kiting abilities. She can be a tough first chase for hunters, as she can use her kit to easily waste their time and abilities.

Even though her containment and decoding are above average she's a poor rescuer with limited assist abilities. Ideally, she shouldn't have to rescue at all.

External Traits and Abilities

Cloak is Faro Lady's main ability. It automatically triggers when within 18 meters of the Hunter increasing exposure by 6.25% every second. This means she can go invisble for a total of 16 seconds. However, moving away from this range will decrease exposure by 5% per second. Moving away with over 40% exposure alerts the Hunter. Reaching 100% exposure or making large movements removes the cloaking effect. Using Flywheel cancels Cloak immediately, which is why she shouldn't bring it.

The longer you remain cloaked, the more time is needed to cloak again, ranging from 5 seconds to 40 seconds. When Exposure is below 40%, dropping Pallets, vaulting through windows, decoding Cipher Machines, and activating the Exit Gate will not end the cloaked effect. The cloaked effect's cooldown is paused when knocked down or immobilized.

Footsteps are concealed while cloaked, but a trail is left behind for 3 seconds. Moving over an existing trail will not create a new trail. The Persona Web trait Air Walk has no effect on this whatsoever.

Faro Lady starts the game with 2 Aromatherapy Canes that she can use to disperse a trail of aroma for 3 seconds. Hunters can't detect the special effects or sound.

For example you can use a cane to pretend you're running through a pallet, but instead you stand still ambushing them with a pallet stun.

You can also throw it out into one direction and then double back like Magician usually does.

You can also do this sneakily when a pallet is being broken.

This can also be applied in two story settings as seen in these examples:

Dexterous allows her to increase a Ciphers progress by 10% instantly if its below 10%. She can target a partially decoded Cipher Machine that is not being actively decoded.

When she decodes another Cipher Machine, the targeted Cipher Machine loses 1% of its Decoding Progress every second while increasing her Decoding Speed by 25%/20%/15%. The Decoding Speed boost decreases as the distance between her and the targeted Cipher Machine increases. This can be used to easily locate your first cipher machine early game too!

Disarray increases Faro Lady's rescuing speed by 60%.

It's an extremely important external trait to keep in mind, because she shouldn't bring Tide Turner and should avoid rescuing if possible.

Persona Web

As mentioned before Faro Lady shouldn't bring Tide or Flywheel so Kneejerk Reflex and Borrowed Time are recommend. Companion effect can come in handy to quickly escape the chair upon rescue so as to cloke again as quickly as possible.

Herd Behavior

Herd Behavior

After being pursued by the Hunter for over 5 seconds, if you leave the Hunter's warning range and the hunter is not in pursuit, the Hunter's outline will be shown to all Survivors for 3 seconds. Cooldown: 80 seconds. The affected Hunter will be immune to this effect for 40 seconds after their outline disappears.

Great Power and Will to Survive also help her kiting and make her more of a nuisance to deal with.

Gameplay

At the start of the match, use Dexterous to find your first cipher and start decoding it immediately. If you get chased first, make sure to use your canes for rotation or to fool the Hunter as shown earlier. If you think the kite will end quickly, save at least one so you have a chance of rebounding after being rescued.

Otherwise, follow the game closely and use Dexterous to steal decoding progress as appropriate. Try not to rescue unless absolutely necessary and you're the only one able to do so.
